Our Services

At Requiem Consultation, we transform homes into dream spaces. Whether you’re looking to expand, reimagine, or completely overhaul your property, our expert team guides you through every step—from initial concept to final handover—with clarity, craftsmanship, and care

Renovations and Extensions

Breathing new life into existing homes or adding meaningful space, our renovations and extensions blend seamless functionality with timeless design.

  • Detailed site surveys and feasibility studies.
  • Bespoke design solutions that respect your home’s character.
  • Efficient project management to minimize disruption.
  • High-quality materials and finishes, sourced responsibly.
  • Full compliance with building regulations and planning permissions.

By the end of your project, you’ll experience enhanced living areas tailored to your lifestyle—whether that’s a bespoke kitchen extension, a bright new sunroom, or an elegant rear addition.

Loft Conversions

Unlock the potential of your attic with our loft conversion expertise. We transform underused roof space into airy, light-filled rooms ideal for bedrooms, studies, or even a home gym.

  • Detailed site surveys and feasibility studies.
  • Bespoke design solutions that respect your home’s character.
  • Efficient project management to minimize disruption.
  • High-quality materials and finishes, sourced responsibly.
  • Full compliance with building regulations and planning permissions.

A loft conversion not only adds valuable square footage but also boosts your property’s market value—all without moving house.

Full Conversions

Need a clean slate? Our full conversion service offers end-to-end remodeling, whether you’re converting a commercial space into residential units or reinventing a detached property from the ground up.

  • Concept-to-completion design and architectural planning.
  • Groundworks, structural work, and utility rerouting.
  • Bespoke interior fit-outs: plumbing, electrical, and HVAC.
  • High-end finishes, joinery, and smart-home integrations.
  • Coordination with specialists: surveyors, engineers, and local authorities.

With our full conversion, you gain total control over layout, style, and functionality—perfect when you’re aiming for a dramatic transformation.
