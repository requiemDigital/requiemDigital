About Me

I’m Requiem, a wedding photographer with over 8 years of experience capturing moments that feel as timeless as they look. Inspired by vintage film and warm, natural light, I tell love stories with a classic-yet-candid style.

My approach is simple: build a connection, blend into the celebration, and let every genuine emotion shine. Whether you’re planning an intimate elopement or a grand barn reception, I’ll be there to document every laugh, tear, and dance move.
